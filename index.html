01 / Who I Am

Product mind.
AI instinct.

I'm a Director of Product who has spent 14 years building at the intersection of complex systems, user behaviour, and emerging technology. My work spans 0-to-1 consumer apps, ERP architecture from scratch, multi-sided platforms, fintech infrastructure, and production AI systems -- not as experiments, but as businesses that ship and grow.

At Edvoy, I own four product verticals simultaneously: a consumer app (1M+ users, 90+ countries), Edvoy Edge (enterprise SaaS), Edvoy Cloud (partner interface), and an internal ERP with 35+ modules. I have shipped four AI systems into production -- RAG knowledge engines, agentic pipelines, LLM chatbots, and GenAI content platforms.

Before Edvoy: Head of Product at Colive (Sattva Group) where I built a 32-module ERP from zero contributing to 550% revenue growth, AVP Products at Univariety (Info Edge), and co-founder plus exit at AbsolutPrep. Earlier career spans Paytm, Lodestar, and Verizon.

Beyond core product work, I take on consulting engagements across e-commerce, home decor, retail, and digital transformation -- building products end-to-end including AI systems, commerce platforms, and SaaS tools for clients across industries.

01
RAG Knowledge Engine · Edvoy Production
Genie -- AI Knowledge Engine
Problem Statement
Students exploring study-abroad options had to navigate hundreds of university pages, visa policy docs, and scholarship portals to get answers. Counsellor queues were overloaded and response times were too slow for a global, always-on student base across 90+ countries.
A production RAG system powering intelligent Q&A across Edvoy's entire knowledge base -- universities, courses, visa, scholarships, FAQs -- serving 1M+ students with sub-second responses and hallucination guardrails.
🎯
Planning & Strategy►
  • Defined RAG architecture vision for multi-domain queries across 90+ countries
  • Mapped knowledge domains: 750+ institutions, visa policies, scholarships, FAQs
  • Prioritised semantic vs fixed-size chunking strategy per content type
  • Aligned on latency SLAs, accuracy thresholds, and hallucination guardrails
🎨
Product Design & UX►
  • Conversational Q&A interface with source attribution and confidence signals
  • Fallback flows for low-confidence retrievals with escalation to counsellors
  • Thumbs up/down feedback loop to continuously retrain retriever quality
  • Admin panel for knowledge ingestion, refresh scheduling, and monitoring
📝
Content & Knowledge Base►
  • Audited and structured 750+ institutional data sources with metadata schema
  • Built content refresh pipelines for live data sync with institutional feeds
  • Wrote system prompts, Genie persona, and tone guidelines
  • Created QA test sets per domain to benchmark retrieval accuracy
⚙
Technical Build & Environments►
  • Vector DB (Pinecone/Weaviate), embedding pipeline, chunking, re-ranking layer
  • LLM integration with context injection and guardrails against hallucination
  • Docker containerisation, CI/CD, staging to production environment promotion
  • Latency optimisation: caching layer, async retrieval, batch embedding jobs
🚀
Deployment & GTM►
  • Phased rollout: internal QA -- counsellor beta -- student-facing production
  • KPIs defined: query resolution rate, CSAT, support ticket deflection %
  • In-app onboarding with seeded example questions to drive adoption
  • Post-launch conversation quality audits and weekly prompt iteration
Support ticket volume reducedSub-second Q&A at 1M+ scale90+ countries coveredAlways-on student support

Personal AI Build · Full-stack Commerce
AI E-Commerce Platform with Order Management System
Problem Statement
Small and mid-size sellers lack access to a modern, AI-enhanced commerce stack that supports cross-border selling, intelligent product discovery, and end-to-end order management -- without the complexity and cost of enterprise platforms. Existing tools either over-engineer the tech or under-deliver on seller experience.
A complete e-commerce platform built from scratch with a fully integrated OMS -- AI-powered discovery, personalised recommendations, dynamic pricing, cross-border seller configuration, and an end-to-end order lifecycle. Built as part of consulting work across retail and e-commerce clients.
🎯
Planning & Architecture►
  • Defined buyer-side and seller-side surfaces including cross-border seller config
  • Mapped OMS modules: order ingestion, status FSM, fulfilment routing, returns, refunds, analytics
  • Headless architecture: decoupled Next.js storefront plus OMS microservice
  • Seller config: multi-currency, cross-border shipping zones, tax rules, customs docs
🎨
Product Design -- Buyer, Seller & Ops►
  • Buyer journey: home -- AI search -- PDP -- cart -- checkout -- confirmation -- tracking
  • Seller dashboard: catalogue management, cross-border pricing config, inventory alerts
  • Seller cross-border config: currency, shipping carrier per region, tax and customs automation
  • OMS operator UI: order queue, exception handling, bulk actions, SLA tracking board
⚙
Technical Build & Environments►
  • Storefront: Next.js with SSR for SEO and Core Web Vitals
  • OMS backend: Node.js microservices, PostgreSQL, Redis for order state management
  • AI: collaborative filtering plus LLM-enhanced semantic search recommendation engine
  • Cross-border: multi-currency engine, shipping zone matrix, customs document generation
  • Docker plus Kubernetes, staging and production environments, full CI/CD pipeline
🚀
Deployment & GTM►
  • Deployed on AWS: ECS plus RDS plus CloudFront CDN with multi-region redundancy
  • Onboarded first merchant as part of consulting engagement -- full seller setup, cross-border config, and catalogue migration
  • GTM: SEO-first content launch, Instagram and Pinterest organic, influencer seeding
  • KPIs: GMV, conversion rate, cart abandonment, OMS SLA adherence, return rate, cross-border order %

Comprehensive HR SaaS with three product modules: Employee Self-Service, Organisation Management, and HR Operations. AI layer powers performance analysis, attrition risk scoring, and an HR policy Q&A chatbot.
🎯
Planning & Module Architecture►
